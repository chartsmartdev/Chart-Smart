# Chart Smart Roadmap

## Phase 1: Foundation - Complete

- Brand and educational principles
- Responsive static structure
- GitHub Pages compatibility
- Responsible learning language

## Phase 2: Decision Lab v0.1 - Complete

- One anonymous Bull Flag scenario
- Trend, pattern, confidence, and action prompts
- Conditional trade plan or no-trade reasoning
- Locked decision summary
- Candle-by-candle price and volume replay
- Separate market outcome and decision-quality grade
- Gentle coaching for supported and unsupported interpretations
- Animated Pattern X-Ray
- Browser-generated learning sound with mute control
- Replayable end screen and skill breakdown

## Phase 3: Scenario Quality

- Expert-reviewed scenario format
- Five to ten varied market structures
- More realistic price, volume, and volatility regimes
- Rubric calibration and confidence scoring
- Touch-friendly chart placement for entry, stop, and target
- Learner usability and accessibility testing

## Phase 4: Progression

- Practice history
- Decision scoring trends
- Discipline, patience, and risk-management streaks
- No-FOMO streak
- Trading Dojo progression
- Investor DNA skill profile

## Phase 5: Personalized Learning

- AI Coach grounded in the scoring rubric
- Adaptive scenario selection
- Targeted lessons based on strengths and weaknesses
- Confidence calibration feedback
- Private beta with a small investment-learning community
