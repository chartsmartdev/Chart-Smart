# Chart Smart Principles

**The market rewards outcomes. Chart Smart rewards decisions.**

## Product Principles

1. Reward disciplined decisions, not lucky outcomes.
2. Teach process before profit.
3. Practice before risking real money.
4. Never imply guaranteed returns.
5. Every feature must help someone become a better decision-maker.
6. Make feedback honest, clear, and useful.
7. Avoid manipulative gambling mechanics.
8. Keep learning loops short, focused, and repeatable.
9. Treat risk management as a core skill, not an advanced feature.
10. Make the product accessible to users who keep sound off or need visual feedback.

## Scoring Principles

A profitable trade can still receive a poor decision score.

A losing trade can still receive a strong decision score.

Decision scoring should eventually consider:

- Trend recognition
- Support and resistance
- Entry quality
- Stop placement
- Risk/reward
- Position sizing
- Patience
- Confirmation
- Confidence calibration
- Discipline

## Sound and Feedback Principles

The future sound system should reinforce learning without resembling a slot machine or encouraging reckless trading.

Potential cues:

- Energetic success cue for strong decisions
- Dramatic warning cue for poor decisions
- Calm cue for good process with a losing outcome
- Uneasy cue for a lucky win with poor process
- Mute and volume controls
- Accessible visual feedback for users with sound off
